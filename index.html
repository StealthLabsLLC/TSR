Meet the Founders

The Social Reserve Mobile Bar Co. was created with a simple goal, to bring people together through thoughtfully designed experiences. Founded by Rachel and Makayla Bryant, what started as hosting gatherings quickly grew into something more.

As a proud military family, they value connection, community, and creating spaces where people can truly relax and enjoy themselves. With backgrounds in hairstyling and event planning, they combine creativity and experience to deliver events that feel seamless, elevated, and intentional.

Today, their focus is simple. To create an experience where every detail is handled and every guest feels welcomed, so you can enjoy your event without the stress.
