About Social Reserve

Built on connection, creativity, and unforgettable experiences.

OUR STORY

More Than a Mobile Bar

The Social Reserve Mobile Bar Co. was created with one simple goal in mind: bringing people together through unforgettable experiences.

Founded by Rachel and Makayla Bryant, our journey started the way many great ideas do, surrounded by friends, family, and a love for creating memorable moments. What began as hosting gatherings and crafting drinks quickly evolved into a vision for something more.

As a proud military family, we understand the importance of connection, community, and creating spaces where people can truly relax and enjoy themselves. Every event we design is rooted in those values.
